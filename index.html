Est. 2019 — Lisbon, Portugal

We make clothes that
outlast trends.

NABEEHA CLOSET was founded from a single conviction: that clothing should be honest. No fast fashion cycles. No trend-chasing. Just pieces designed with the patience and craft they deserve.

We work with a small network of artisan manufacturers across Portugal and Japan, chosen for their commitment to traditional techniques and ethical practices. Every garment passes through fewer than eight hands before it reaches you.

We produce in small batches — typically under 200 units per style. When something sells out, it doesn't come back. This isn't manufactured scarcity; it's honest production.

01 —

Slow Fashion

Two collections per year, not four. We'd rather get it right than get it out quickly.

02 —

Ethical Production

All manufacturing partners are audited annually. Living wages, safe conditions, no exceptions.

03 —

Sustainable Materials

90% of our fabrics are organic, recycled, or deadstock. The other 10% is something we're working on.

04 —

Lifetime Repairs

Every piece comes with a lifetime repair promise. Send it back, we'll fix it. Free, forever.
